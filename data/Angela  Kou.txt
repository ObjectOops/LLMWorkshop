Research StatementProf. Kou's work is at the interface between condensed matter physics and quantum information science, with a focus on topological materials. Her research uses superconducting circuits and scanned probe microscopies to build and investigate topological materials.
Post-Doctoral Research OpportunitiesWe are looking for postdocs to work on superconducting qubits and/or sensing of topological materials.
Graduate Research OpportunitiesWe are looking for students to work on building superconducting circuits for sensing topological materials.
