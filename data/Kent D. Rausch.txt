Research Interests
Effects of grain genetics and quality characteristics on processing efficiency.
Investigation of heat transfer fouling phenomena applied to grain processes.
Membrane filtration research applied to grain processing systems to improve nutrient recovery and reduce water and energy consumption.
Development and implementation of grain dryer technology suitable for smallholder farmers and villages.
Use of hermetic storage technology to reduce postharvest losses of stored grains.
